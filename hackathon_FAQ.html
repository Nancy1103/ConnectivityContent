Frequently Asked Questions

Arriving, Sleeping, Eating

  • Where is my hotel confirmation? Hotel confirmations will be emailed to you directly closer to the event. If you received a confirmation email from us on May 20 then you are definitely registered. If you did not receive a confirmation email, please contact your market manager.
  • Are meals provided? More information about meals is on the Hackathon Page. If you have dietary restrictions, please work with your market manager to get in contact with the hackathon organizing team and we will do what we can.
  • Airport Transfer More information here later.

Hackathon Event

  • How much time will we have? You will have the time between when we start the hackathon till when the judging begins. Some teams will work late into the night to get as far as they can before the judging, but this is not required.
  • Can we prepare? You are encouraged to read up on the APIs you plan to use. You are welcome to write sample code using an EQC account to which you have access. The experience you gain will help you make more progress during the hackathon. You are expected to write the code for your project during the hackathon.

Questions of Other Categories

  • Visa Invitation Letter If you require an invitation letter to apply for a visa, please contact your market manager and we will work with them to get you an invitation letter.
