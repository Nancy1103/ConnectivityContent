Hackathon Themes & User Stories

Feel free to pick and hack any problem, which represents relevant, creative, high business value scenarios and challenges your organization is handling or would like to tackle in the future.

For guidance and seed ideas, below are a few use cases we are proposing. Most of these scenarios can be accomplished using the powerful set of APIs Expedia will be exposing for this event.

In addition to these, we would also encourage using other public, open-source tools, platforms to accomplish your goal.

Lodging

  1. As a Hotel Partner, reduce time to load and update products on Expedia, eliminate manual errors, save cost by automatically creating new Room Types and Rate Plans.
  2. As a Hotel Partner, identify any differentials and verify product mapping for hotel room types and rate plans loaded on Expedia.
  3. Fast and effective Promotion creations!
  4. Get promotions score to better create, update promotions on Expedia.
  5. Improve content quality for the hotels with minimal manual intervention.
  6. Effectively manage hotel images.
  7. Fully automate end-to-end onboarding of new hotels and save time and effort to get my hotels live on Expedia!
  8. Automate and optimize Value-Add Promotions management.
  9. Search for Value-Add promotions available.
  10. Discover newly added properties to my account, so I can start mapping process.
  11. Get all RoomTypes and Rateplans for properties in Expedia system, to create structure in ChannelManager tools and do mapping (replacing PARR).
  12. Take actions to make your availability and rates competitive, by getting supplier scores for the inventory you manage.
  13. Get scoring for hotel content so you can improve content quality, build trends, make recommendations.
  14. Get demand data for hotels for better inventory management.
  15. Get insights into lost opportunities (for example: customer viewed the hotel but did not book) to take remedial actions.

Event Guidelines

  • Come with ideas not completed code. We want you to brainstorm within your company about how you would use our APIs in your solution, which means you may need to write some code to understand our APIs to take full advantage of working with our engineers. But don't go beyond tinkering with our APIs. This is meant to be a fun event and we want all teams to hack on site.
  • Team must demo a functional application to be eligible for prizes.
  • All applications will be judged against the criteria published here.
  • Teams will have access to Expedia engineering and API experts for guidance and help.
  • Teams will have access to Expedia mentors for guidance on areas like business use cases, Value add, Impact, Demo presentations.
  • Hack. Meet new people. Revolutionize Travel. Have fun while doing it!
